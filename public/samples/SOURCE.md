# Sample contract sources

The `*.txt` files in this folder are real commercial contracts from the
**Contract Understanding Atticus Dataset (CUAD) v1**, published by The Atticus
Project and licensed under **Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 (CC BY 4.0)**.

CUAD sources its contracts from public filings on the U.S. SEC EDGAR system.
They are included here, unmodified, only as realistic sample data for the
workspace's contract picker — no ownership is claimed.

- Dataset: https://www.atticusprojectai.org/cuad
- Record: https://zenodo.org/records/4595826
- Paper: https://arxiv.org/abs/2103.06268

| `endorsement-agreement.txt` | Endorsement | HOLIDAYRVSUPERSTORESINC_04_15_2002-EX-10.13-ENDORSEMENT AGREEMENT |

The bundled demo NDA (`data/sample-contract.ts`) is hand-written for this project
and is not part of CUAD.
